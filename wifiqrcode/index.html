Wi-Fi QR Code

Help your guests join your Wi-Fi network quickly!
You can generate a QR Code image that enables your guests quickly connect to your Wi-Fi spot with Wi-Fi QR Code.
Wi-Fi QR Code keep your secret and privacy. This app does not send neither your SSIDs nor passwords over the internet

Features

  • Printing QR Code with your AirPrint ready printers.
  • Free and No Ads
  • Supports hidden mode (stealth) Wi-Fi network
